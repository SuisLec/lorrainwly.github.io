About

My name is Lorrain Wei（魏龙咏）. I am a Robotics Engineering undergraduate at USTB, advised by Professor Jin Wu.

My research interests include embodied AI, world models, and robot pose estimation.

In the long run, I hope to pursue a PhD in robotics and contribute to robotic systems for space exploration and intelligent surgical robotics that enhance human healthcare.

Outside of academics, I am also passionate about photography, which shapes how I observe the world beyond engineering.

Institution USTB (University of Science and Technology Beijing)
Based in Beijing, China
